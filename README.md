# Clothing_Dashboard_Project
Excited to share my Clothing Store Data Analysis Dashboard, an interactive analytics solution built to help retail stakeholders monitor sales performance, analyze customer behavior, and identify growth opportunities.

This dashboard converts raw retail data into meaningful insights that support smarter, data-driven business decisions.

Key Highlights :

Primary KPIs
Total Sales Revenue – Track overall performance and identify high- and low-performing periods
Total Orders & Quantity Sold – Measure demand trends and sales volume
Average Order Value (AOV) – Understand customer spending patterns

Visuals Included :

Sales Trend Analysis: Monthly and yearly sales trends to identify peak and slow periods
Category-wise Sales Performance: Revenue comparison across product categories (e.g., tops, bottoms, accessories)
Sub-category Analysis: Deeper insights into product-level performance
Top-Selling Products: Identify best-performing items contributing most to revenue
Bottom-Performing Products: Spot underperforming items for optimization
Customer Gender Distribution: Sales contribution by gender
Age Group Analysis: Understand purchasing patterns across age segments
Regional / Store-wise Sales: Performance comparison across locations

 Dashboard Features :

Interactive slicers and filters (Year, Month, Category, Gender, Payment Mode)
Dynamic charts and KPIs that update instantly with user selections
Drill-down capabilities for detailed product and customer insights
Sparklines to visualize quick sales trends
Cleaned and structured dataset ensuring data accuracy and consistency
User-friendly dashboard layout for easy interpretation by business users

Learning Outcomes :

Through this project, I gained hands-on experience in:
Leveraging Excel’s advanced features (Pivot Tables, Conditional Formatting, Dynamic Charts).
Transforming raw data into actionable intelligence.
This project demonstrates how data analytics can drive better inventory management, customer targeting, and revenue growth in the retail domain
